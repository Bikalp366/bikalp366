Definitions

Vegan
A diet that excludes all animal products, including dairy, eggs, and meat.
Gluten-Free
Food that does not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye.
Organic
Food produced without the use of synthetic chemicals, fertilizers, or pesticides.
